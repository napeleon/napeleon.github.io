Aracne Summary

Arachne was a talented weaver, because of her talent she become famous in the whole Greece. However, because of her fame she became more arrogance, that she challenged goddess Athena for a competition on weaving. Athena accept her challenge. And they start to weave, Arachne was fine but goddess Athena was fast. After that they finished, A thena made an cloth that shows the strength of gods, while Arachne displayed the failures of gods. Athena insulted, she made Arachne an small spider. Now she can weave forever.
